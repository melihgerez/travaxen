About the Prototype Product Image

General Design

When designing the prototype device, the edges were softened for ergonomics. This aimed to avoid any potential deformations. A protruding structure was created for the cover at the top, allowing for easy installation.

Front Design

A gap was left on the front of the device for cable connections from the external power supply. A space was also left for a power light and a power button.

Rear Design

On the back of the device, a reset button and a USB port for updating or installing software were provided.

Side Designs

Because the device will be in use for long hours, five separate, thin, and long cooling fins were carefully designed on the sides.*

*This device is only a prototype of the actual product. If necessary, small fans can be added to the cooling fins as part of the product's design.
