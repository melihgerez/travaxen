About the Prototype Product Image

General Design

When designing our prototype device, we softened the edges for ergonomics. This aimed to avoid any potential deformations. We created a protruding structure for the cover at the top, allowing for easy installation.

Front Design

We left a gap on the front of the device for cable connections from the external power supply. We also left a space for a power light and a power button.

Rear Design

On the back of the device, we provided a reset button and a USB port for updating or installing software.

Side Designs

Because the device will be in use for long hours, we carefully designed five separate, thin, and long cooling fins on the sides.*

*This device is only a prototype of the actual product. If necessary, we can add small fans to the cooling fins as part of the product's design.
