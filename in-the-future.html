Hospital Integration

Thanks to Travaxen, an ambulance approaching a hospital now provides early warning to the hospital, informing it of the patient's health and needs.

If the patient's condition is critical, the time needed for rescue is available.

With This Feature:

  • Hospital emergency room can prepare in advance
  • Required equipment and personnel are kept ready
  • Critical patients have a higher chance of survival
  • Time loss is minimized
