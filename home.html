üöÄ Who Are We?

As a young team passionate about technology, we aim to create solutions that save lives in traffic.

Travaxen isn't just a project; it's a movement aimed at preventing loss of life by raising awareness among drivers and emergency responders.

By combining software, electronics, and traffic safety, we've designed a system that's both simple and practical.

When emergency vehicles approach, drivers will no longer rely solely on sirens. Our system will warn them in advance.

Our goal is to use technology to simplify and protect human life. Travaxen is just the first step.
